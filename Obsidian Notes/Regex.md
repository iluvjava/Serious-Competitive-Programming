### Regular Expressions
* If $A$, $B$ are regular expressions, then so are: 
	* $A\cup B$ 
	* $(AB)$
	* $A*$
* Things that cannot be represented by Regular Expression
	* Palidromes
	* Strings with equal number of 0's and 1's 
	* Matched Parenthesis
